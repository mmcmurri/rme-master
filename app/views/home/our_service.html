What about inspections?

Great Question. We asked the pro’s....

So, what are you guys looking for when you’re up there?
-Cracked or broken shingles
-Signs of water damage
-Anything else that might lead us to believe the water barrier or decking could be damaged
How often do you find critical damage?
-It depends on when the roof was last replaced, the regional climate and other nuances. Usually less frequent with well kept homes. Sometimes people notice water spots inside their home, or have leaks
What do you do when you discover damage?
-We usually inform the homeowner what we’ve found to make them aware there might be additional costs after the tear down to replace the barrier, decking and even the insulation if water has gotten through
Does this affect the estimate?
-Well, when we start we are pricing out the roofing replacement: shingles, water barrier, etc.. We need to be able to see under the existing shingles to asses what the damage is and what needs fixing. The cost to fix things like decking are always additional because we will only fix the decking that need to be replaced. We don’t know what the is until tear-down.
So, you quote for a roof replacement and then whatever damages you find during tear-down are added on top?
-Correct. Its nearly impossible for us to know how damaged the under-roof is just from physically assessing it. The only advantage is that we can give the homeowner a heads up that their is evidence of potential damage

Roof inspections identify potential issues. Once you’ve selected a contractor for your roof replacement, a representative will meet you for a walkthrough.

Our Service...

Roofing Made Easy

In three easy steps you can have your roof replaced. Simply confirm the booking with one of our online payment methods and the contractor, product and disposal bin will all show up at your home ready for installation on the confirmed date.

After confirming the booking, we will notify the contractor and they will swing by to take a quick look at your roof for any possible damage. They will report back to us with the results and we will work with you to keep the situation under control.

No more sales people, no more surprises. Just Roofing Made Easy.

Discovering Roof Damage

In the event that one of our contractors discovers damage to your under-roof, they will notify us of the severity and are required to submit pictures to our customer service centre for handling. We resolve these issues immediately upon receipt and guarantee resolution within 24 hours.

During the walkthrough, a contractor will speak to you about the damage and show you what the issues are. We will work with the contractor to identify what the cost is to fix the damage, and relay that information to you immediately so you can make the final decision. 

Site Damage & Complaints

We are the centralized source for all feedback, complaints and disputes. If your roofers have caused material damage to your home or property, submit photo evidence of the damage and complete our “Dispute Form”.

We will handle the dispute on your behalf,  and reimburse you for any damages while you sit back and enjoy the completed work.
